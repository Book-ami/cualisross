Meet the Suffolk Public Library!

The Suffolk Public Library serves the city and county of Suffolk in southeastern Virginia in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States of America. Suffolk Public Library boasts three brick-and mortar locations throughout Suffolk as well as mobile outreach services including a Library2Go bookmobile and pop-up locations throughout the county. Its three branches are:

  1. Morgan Memorial
  2. North Suffolk
  3. Chuckatuck

Suffolk Public Library’s vision is to serve as "a hub for community connection and to spark change” in Suffolk, Virginia. Its mission is to connect individuals and groups to experiences, information, and resources, prioritize equitable access to opportunities, and serve the unique needs of a diverse community.

The Suffolk Public Library is community focused, nimble, inclusive, impactful and adventurous which means that it has something for everyone beyond traditional book lending services. There are regular classes and events for young people from babies to teens, both in-person and virtually as well as for adults and senior adults. While Suffolk Public library endeavors to spark the interest of all it patrons through the Collection of Things and other initiatives such as the Seed Library and Spice of the Month kits. Suffolk Public Library cares for the concerns of the community by providing resources for job hunting and professional development, guides to community resources and partners with community organizations to meet the needs of their Suffolk neighbors.

The Suffolk Public Library book collection is over 160,000 strong. E-books and other e-resources such as audiobooks and movies are available through Hoopla and Libby (just to start with).It is easy to search Suffolk Public Library's catalog through its encompassing Quick Search. Alternatively, you specify the type of search (keyword, browse, phrase, exact or advanced). Each catalog record result has immediately garners a quick display providing the name of the title, author, publication year, a brief blurb, publisher, date published, description, availability, call number and, when available, a Google Books preview. Three buttons are also available to produce the specific availability (which library holds the title), full display (includes edition, subjects, ISBN, current holds and ratings) and option to place a request (to another Suffolk Public Library location or its affiliates).
